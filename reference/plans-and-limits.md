# Plans & limits

Launch Flow has a free plan plus two paid tiers. Paid plans include a 7-day free trial. All charges are billed in USD every 30 days through Shopify.

| Feature                            | Free        | Starter ($14/mo) | Growth ($29/mo) |
| ---------------------------------- | ----------- | ---------------- | --------------- |
| Scheduled events                   | 1 at a time | 10               | Unlimited       |
| Price changes with snapshots       | ✅           | ✅                | ✅               |
| Auto-rollback when promotions end  | —           | ✅                | ✅               |
| Theme & page publishing            | —           | —                | ✅               |
| Email notifications                | —           | —                | ✅               |
| Audit log retention                | 7 days      | 30 days          | Full            |
| Free trial                         | —           | 7 days           | 7 days          |

You can change plans at any time from the billing screen inside the app. Upgrading takes effect immediately; downgrading may limit how many launches you can keep scheduled at once.
