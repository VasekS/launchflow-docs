# Email notifications

{% hint style="info" %}
Available on the **Growth** plan.
{% endhint %}

Launch Flow emails you when key events happen so you're never guessing whether a launch fired. Notifications are sent for events such as a launch going live and a launch rolling back.

* Delivery is handled through Resend, Launch Flow's email provider.
* Make sure the email address on your account is one you monitor around sale times.

{% hint style="success" %}
**Check your dashboard too.** Even without email, every launch's status is always visible in the Launch Flow dashboard and the [audit log](audit-log.md).
{% endhint %}
